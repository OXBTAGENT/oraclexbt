2. Cross-Market Pattern Recognition

The agent continuously scans order books across multiple platforms to identify statistical anomalies.

  • Volume Anomaly Detection: Identifies sudden liquidity inflows (e.g., >300% volume 1h) preceding major price moves.
  • Mean Reversion: Tracks overextended market moves triggered by "fake news" or panic, executing counter-trades when probability deviates >2σ from the baseline.
  • Liquidity Trap Avoidance: Analyzing depth charts to ensure exit liquidity before entering positions.

3. Arbitrage Execution Grid

A low-latency execution engine seeking risk-free or statistical arbitrage opportunities.

  • Spatial Arbitrage: Exploiting price differences for the same event across platforms (e.g., "Will Trump Win?" trading at 52¢ on Polymarket vs 58¢ on Kalshi).
  • Spread Capturing: Simultaneously buying "YES" and "NO" orders when the combined probability is < 100%.
  • Latency Arbitrage: Front-running slow-updating UI prices using direct blockchain/API data feeds.
